At Princeton

I'm currently a graduate student researcher with Professor Ali Yazdani in the Princeton Nanoscale Microscopy Laboratory using high-resolution scanning probe microscopy techniques to study exotic phases of matter in quantum materials. My recent work has focused on using scanning tunneling microscopy (STM) to understand the correlated and topological phases of magic-angle twisted bilayer graphene (MATBG).
