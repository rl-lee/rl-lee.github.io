About me

Welcome! My name is Ryan and I am a third year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Physics at Princeton University. I'm an experimental condensed matter physicist broadly interested in emergent phenomena in strongly correlated quantum materials. I'm also very passionate about diversity and inclusion outreach and am actively involved in the Equity, Diversity, and Inclusion initiative at Princeton Physics. Keep scrolling to read more!

Research

Princeton University

I'm currently a graduate student researcher with Professor Ali Yazdani in the Princeton Nanoscale Microscopy Laboratory using high-resolution scanning probe microscopy techniques to understand the nature of quantum phases in materials.
